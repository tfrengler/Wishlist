To add or edit wishes you need to log in (via the menu).

If you're not logged in you can only view the wishlists.

After logging in, and you've opened your list, you'll have three
buttons in the upper right corner.

To change the content of a wish you need to first click on
Enable editing in the upper right corner.

IMPORTANT: Don't forget to click the Save-button when you're
done! Otherwise your changes won't actually be saved.
